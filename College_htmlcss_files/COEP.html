Location: PUNE, MAHARASTRA

College of Engineering, Pune is an autonomous Government institute and is affiliated with UniPune (Savitribai Phule Pune University). The College was founded in 1854 and is recognised by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA). In the Outlook - ICARE ranking for 2021, COEP is spotted at rank 18 among the Government Engineering colleges. The College offers various UG, PG and PhD courses in Management, Engineering and Technology streams. Students can also pursue one year PG Diploma programmes, selection to which are made through an online test/ interview. Some of the top recruiting companies that have visited the COEP campus for placements include Deloitte, Hyundai Motors, Infosys, among others. The university also awards scholarships to meritorious students through various schemes.
